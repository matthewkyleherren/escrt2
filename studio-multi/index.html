An identity to launch the new architecture practice, Studio MULTI – for now, and tomorrow.

Central to the identity is a typeface that has distinct ‘ink traps’ within the tight corners of the letters. As Seth, one of the partners, observed, “this references the way we look at buildings, which reveal details the closer you get to them”.

Studio MULTI each brings 20+ years of experience to the practice and are not interested in using old projects from previous roles as their portfolio. So, the website has been designed to evolve with them over time.

Its initial state allows for multiple content types, each presented in a single hierarchy. Once they have completed several projects, the website will separate the content types allowing for a different user experience.

Website – stage 1

We took an elemental approach with colour, where the colours are only seen on their own and never side-by-side.

It allows Studio MULTI to communicate with emotion rather than being used just as a brand signal.
